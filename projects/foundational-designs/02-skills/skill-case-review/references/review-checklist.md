# Case Review Checklist

Use this checklist to evaluate a support case systematically.

## SLA Compliance

- [ ] Is the case within its priority-level response target?
  - Critical: 1 hour | High: 4 hours | Medium: 1 day | Low: 3 days
- [ ] Has the customer received at least one substantive response?
- [ ] Is the case assigned to the right team/person for its category?

## Customer Context

- [ ] Is the customer tier reflected in the case priority?
  - Enterprise customers should default to `high` priority minimum
- [ ] Are there other open cases for this customer? (check for patterns)
- [ ] Is the customer in a trial period or recently onboarded?

## Case Quality

- [ ] Is the case subject clear and specific?
- [ ] Is there enough information to reproduce or understand the issue?
- [ ] Are internal notes documenting investigation steps?
- [ ] Has a knowledge article been linked if one exists?

## Resolution Path

- [ ] Is there a clear next action identified?
- [ ] If pending on customer, has a follow-up reminder been set?
- [ ] If escalated, is the escalation reason documented?
- [ ] Could this case inform a new or updated knowledge article?

## Red Flags

- [ ] Case open > 7 days without resolution
- [ ] Customer has replied but no agent response within SLA
- [ ] Priority mismatch (enterprise customer with low priority)
- [ ] Multiple back-and-forth exchanges without progress
- [ ] Case reassigned more than twice
